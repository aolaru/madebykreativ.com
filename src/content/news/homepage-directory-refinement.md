The homepage was simplified to reduce repeated Kreativ Tools promotion, make the ecosystem easier to scan, and keep the emphasis on all four properties together rather than on one launch message.

The updated structure brings the free guide, paid toolkit, core properties, project directory, and recent updates into a clearer order. Visitors can now understand the site as an ecosystem rather than a single-product announcement page.

This also made room for the project map and product cards to carry more of the navigation work, with individual project logos and cleaner calls to action.
