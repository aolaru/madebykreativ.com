<a class="cta" href="https://howmuchmusic.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">howmuchmusic.com</a> has launched as a new music-focused project, expanding the ecosystem with a dedicated destination around music discovery, value, and practical listening context.

How Much Music sits in the discovery-project category because it is less about utility software and more about giving music exploration a clearer frame. The project focuses on listening decisions, reference value, and useful context for people who spend time with music seriously.

The Made by Kreativ directory now links it alongside the other public ecosystem properties so users can move between creative assets, tools, and discovery work from one place.
