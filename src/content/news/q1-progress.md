The ecosystem continues to expand with curated typography references, cinematic sound resources, and practical WordPress utilities built for long-term use.

Q1 work focused on making the core properties more distinct: Kreativ Font for typography discovery, Kreativ Sound for production-ready audio resources, and Kreativ WP for practical WordPress utility work.

That separation matters because each property serves a different workflow. The Made by Kreativ site now keeps those roles visible through project profiles, related updates, and a shared ecosystem directory.

The next layer is connecting those properties more clearly through updates, product pages, and practical cross-links. That way a visitor interested in one area can still understand the broader set of Kreativ projects without needing to piece it together from separate sites.
